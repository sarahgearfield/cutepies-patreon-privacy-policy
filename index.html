Privacy Policy for Pinterest Image Automation

Effective Date: October 13, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our service to automate image posting to Pinterest.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our service:

  • Pinterest Account Information: We may access basic account information from Pinterest, such as your Pinterest username, boards, and pins.
  • Images: Images generated by your system and uploaded to Pinterest through our automation service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • Automating Image Posting: To automatically upload images to Pinterest based on your configuration and preferences.
  • Board Management: To post images to specific boards as specified in your settings.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

We do not store any personal data permanently. The images and metadata are processed in real-time to be uploaded to Pinterest and are not retained on our servers.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information during data transmission, such as encryption.

4. Your Rights

You can request:

  • Data Access: Information about the data we have access to via Pinterest.
  • Data Deletion: Complete removal of all automated posts.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
